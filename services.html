Our Services

What we build, and how we build it.

Renovations

From full basement suites to high-end kitchen and bathroom remodels, we take on the renovations that require real craftsmanship. Whether you're a homeowner building your dream space or an investor adding value to a property, we bring the same standard to every scope.

  • Full basement developments — legal suites, homeowner finishing, investor projects
  • Kitchen remodels
  • Bathroom remodels
  • Large-scale residential renovation

Framing

Where our expertise started. Framing is the backbone of every build — and understanding it from the inside out is what sets us apart. That structural knowledge carries through every project we touch, regardless of scope.

  • New residential builds
  • Additions and structural extensions
  • Structural framing repairs
  • Multi-unit and infill projects

General Contracting

If you have a residential project and need someone to take the wheel, we manage the process from start to finish. From sourcing the right trades to keeping the schedule on track, we're the single point of accountability.

  • Full project facilitation and scheduling
  • Trade sourcing and coordination
  • Owner's representative for new builds
  • Guidance for lot purchases and ground-up builds
